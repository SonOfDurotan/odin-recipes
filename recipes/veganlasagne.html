Vegan Lasagne

You can have cheesy lasagne as a vegan too. Here is my recipe for vegan lasagne using entirely homemade "cheeses."

Ingredients:

  • Lasagne noodles
  • Cashews
  • San Marzano tomatoes
  • Italian spices (Oregano, dried basil, garlic powder)
  • Spinach
  • Red lentils

Steps:

  1. Cook red lentils
  2. Add cashews to food processor to make the "ricotta"
  3. Assemble the lasagne noodles
  4. Cover and bake for 40 minutes
