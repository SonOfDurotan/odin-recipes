Masoor Dal (Instant Pot)

Masoor dal is a staple of Indian households of every class and region. Here is my recipe using an Instant Pot instead of a traditional pressure cooker.

Ingredients:

  • Masoor lentils
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices (Coriander, Cumin, Red chili powder)
  • Garlic and ginger

Steps:

  1. Saute garlic and ginger
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, and water as needed
  3. Add lentils and cook on "High" for 5 minutes
  4. Let sit for 5 minutes before releasing remaining pressure manually
